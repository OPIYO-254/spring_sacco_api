Our Data Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Data Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when you use our mobile app.

2. Information we collect

a. User Account Information
When you sign up for Sojrel Sacco, we collect the following information:
  • Email Address
  • Password
  • Biometric authentication data (fingerprint and faceID)
b. Limited Access to Sacco Server

Sojrel Sacco Ltd does not gather any additional information from users other than what is essential for providing financial services, specifically loan products of different categories. The app accesses and displays only the registered details from the Sacco server.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide for the following purposes:
  • User authentication and authorization
  • Providing access to registered details from the Sacco server
  • Issuing financial services, including loan products as outlines here

4. Data Security

Sojrel Sacco Ltd takes the security of your information seriously. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties unless required by law or necessary for the provision of financial services.

6. Consent

By using the Sojrel Sacco mobile app, you consent to the collection, use, and sharing of your information as described in this Data Privacy Policy.

7. User Rights
As a user, you have the right to:
