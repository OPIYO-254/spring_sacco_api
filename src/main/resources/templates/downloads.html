Become a Member

While everyone is eligible to join Sojrel Sacco, it is only members who meet the requirements stated here that qualify to become members.

Ready to join?

Download the application form shown fill and send it to sojrelsaccomanager@gmail.com together with other required documents as stated below.

  • A copy of National ID Card/Passport.
  • A Passport size photograph.
  • KRA Certificate.
  • A duly filled application form.

You can also visit our offices at Jeniffer Riria Hub, 2nd floor with the required documents to get your account opened.
