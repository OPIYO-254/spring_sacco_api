Our Beginnings

Sojrel Sacco, is your trusted partner on the path to financial success. Established in 2021, we are a member-owned financial institution dedicated to helping individuals like you achieve your financial goals. The society was founded with a clear mission in mind: To uplift life in the society through provision of affordable financial services and ideas. What sets us apart is our commitment to inclusivity. Although initiated by employees of Sojrel Traders, we are open to all members of the public. We believe that everyone deserves access to financial support and advice, regardless of their background or affiliations.

Our Location

Our headquarters are nestled in the heart of Nakuru, a city known for its vibrant economy and rich culture. You can find us in the prestigious Jennifer Riria Hub building, where our dedicated team operates from the second floor. This central location ensures that we are easily accessible to all our members and allows us to serve the Nakuru and entire Kenyan community effectively.

Our Services

Sojrel Sacco offers a wide range of financial services and products designed to help you achieve financial stability and success. Our key offerings include:
